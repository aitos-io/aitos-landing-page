01  —  The Gap

The world has abundant data.
Very little of it is sovereign.

Billions of devices, machines and AI agents digitize the physical world every second — energy, mobility, industry, the environment, human activity. Yet almost none of that data can be verified, owned, or fairly monetized.

✕
No proof of origin

Which real device produced this data? Today, nothing can prove it.

✕
No proof of integrity

Altered, duplicated, replayed — and no one can tell the difference.

✕
No control

Owners cannot govern who accesses their data, or for what.

✕
No privacy

Once shared, data escapes its owner — forever.

✕
No reward

Value flows to platforms — not to the people and machines who contribute.

Data is everywhere. Trust, ownership and value flow are not.
